Research Areas

Our department is centred on innovation in the following areas. We also support interdisciplinary work linking ICT to health, agriculture, and education.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Adaptive systems, neural networks, and intelligent agents for real-world impact.

Cybersecurity & Privacy

Cryptography, network defence, digital forensics, and data protection policy.

Data Science & Big Data Analytics

Statistical modelling, predictive analytics, and large-scale data infrastructure.

Software Engineering & HCI

Agile methodologies, DevOps, usability research, and human-centred design.
