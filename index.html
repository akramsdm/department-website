About the Department

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, faith-based education in computing that equips students with the skills, ethics, and vision to lead in a rapidly changing digital world.

Our Vision

To be the leading centre for technological innovation and informatics research in East Africa — recognised for academic excellence and community impact.

Department History

Founded to meet Uganda's growing demand for technology professionals, the department has grown into a hub for digital innovation, research, and industry collaboration.
