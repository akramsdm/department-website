Academic Programs

Undergraduate

BSc · 3–4 Years

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Focus: Core computing theory, algorithms & systems

Builds a strong foundation in programming, data structures, operating systems, and software engineering principles.

BSc · 3–4 Years

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Focus: IT systems, networks & business applications

Covers IT infrastructure, networking, database administration, and the management of technology in organisations.

BSc · 3–4 Years

Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

Focus: Software design, development & quality

Emphasises the full software lifecycle — from requirements and architecture to testing, deployment, and agile practices.

Postgraduate

MSc · 1.5–2 Years

Master of Science in Computer Science

Focus: Advanced computing & research

Deepens theoretical and applied knowledge in algorithms, distributed systems, and emerging computing paradigms.

MSc · 1.5–2 Years

Master of Science in Cybersecurity

Focus: Network defense, cryptography & policy

Prepares graduates to design, implement, and manage secure systems in enterprise and government environments.

MSc · 1.5–2 Years

Master of Science in Data Science & AI

Focus: Machine learning, analytics & AI ethics

Trains data scientists to extract insight from large datasets using statistical modelling, ML, and responsible AI practices.

Category Requirement
Undergraduate Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with passes in Mathematics and English, plus Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) or equivalent. Entry test may be required.
Postgraduate (Taught) Bachelor's degree in Computing, IT, or a related field with a minimum of a Second Class degree. Two academic references required.
Postgraduate (Research) As above, plus a research proposal and an interview with the supervisory team. A First Class or Upper Second Class degree is preferred.
International Applicants Proof of English language proficiency (IELTS 6.0 or equivalent) and certified equivalence of prior qualifications from the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB).
Mature Entry Applicants aged 25+ with relevant professional experience may apply through the mature entry route. Portfolio or written statement required.
How to apply: All applications are submitted through the Bugema University online portal. Ensure all supporting documents (transcripts, ID, references) are uploaded before submission.
